iFloat24 format
===============

+0 		Mantissa 0 	00-FF
+1 		Mantissa 1 	00-FF
+2 		Mantissa 2  00-7F

+3 		Unused 		Bit 7
		Sign 		Bit  6
		Exponent	Bits 0..5

if exponent == int, then integer. Integer range is +/- 7FFFFF = +/- 8,388,607

float max = 7FFFFF * 2 ^ 31 = 	1.8 x 10^16
	  min = 1 * 2^-16 = 		2.3 x 10^-10
